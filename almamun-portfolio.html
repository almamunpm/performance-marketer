My creative framework

Every ad I write follows a battle-tested structure. Hook → Pain → Promise → Proof → CTA. It's not magic — it's methodology.

01
Interrupt — The hook must stop the scroll in 1.5 seconds. Pattern disruption, bold claim, or mirror statement.
02
Agitate — Name the pain with precision. Vague pain = low CTR. Specific pain = high intent.
03
Promise — A single, believable outcome. Not "grow your business" — "cut your CPL by 40%."
04
Prove — Number, testimonial, or before/after. One proof point crushes skepticism.
05
Close — CTA tied to the hook's promise. Never generic "Learn More" — always specific.
